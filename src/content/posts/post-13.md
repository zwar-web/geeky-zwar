Season 6 in Dark War Survival signals a huge change to the gameplay and alliance collaboration.  It has caused a huge division with players, its love hate thing. Personally, I feel it's a step in the right direction, promoting teamwork rather than a dictatorship. Its not all good theres the tile capture to march thing...&#x20;

### Season 6 Overview

You will be allocated a team, mixed from your own server and at least one other. Your opponents will have the same mixed server collaboration. The key here is the attitudes of the dominant forces. You will need more than one strong team to grab a victory in Season 6, the side that unifies its forces and works in partnership will win through.&#x20;

Gameplay

The basic gameplay changes for S6 include a new tile walking system, Portals, territory Gates and Strongholds. You will have to grab tiles in sequential order to progress across the map.  If you don't connect tiles, you cannot seize objectives, such as the Oasis, Strongholds and Pyramids.&#x20;
