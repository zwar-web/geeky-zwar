_**The best event in the Dark War Survival calendar**_ - yes, even beats State Of Supremacy for sheer fun factor! However, organising teams and working out objectives used to be a real pain - but now we have the Z-war BGB planner to help make this a simple process.&#x20;

* Allocating players to Team A or B
* Allocate Starters and Reserves
* Plan Objective Teams
* Reports - to Discord and In-game email&#x20;

​

The BGB Planner tools work with your existing roster. Just duplicate the in-game team list, get the 30 allocated first, then flip out the reserves and start allocating to the event objectives. You can pick how many refineries to go after 1-6, then allocate players to hold (if you wish) or move out to seize the tactical advantages from the Hospitals and Military Bases. These are always good battles until the event decider triggers. If you don't know yet, the golden balls - or more correctly, the Rare Earth Alloy objectives score massive points and usually decide the event outcome. This is why it's vital to get your teams organised and deployed. Planning and Communication!

## BGB Team Planner

There are various ways to access, create and edit the teams and objectives in the BGB Event planner, but when we first enter the option, we get the interactive map view - but first, allocate the players into their A and B teams. This is quick to do from your existing roster - not added yet ? Best head over to the getting started post...

![DWS BGB team allocation ](/images/bgb-allocate-teams.png)

> Teams+ allocates the players to their respective teams, theres also an mini objective dash so you can scan through for players you've missed or might want to reallocate.

This option allows you to do both teams at once, just add the players from the in-game register (if only we had an API to link into !) We will allocate who's reserve and starter later. Just get the 30 players added to each team. Then click Team A, and obviously later duplicate this process for Team B.

​

![DWS BGB Starters and reserves](/images/bgb-allocate-starters-reserves.png)

You should note that you will need to make sure that you have a mx of 20 starters, the counter goes red until you allocate sufficient reserves.  Do this for both teams.&#x20;

### BGB Objectives&#x20;

Now you have your teams allocated, this is where your strategy comes into play, allocating your players to objectives, initially the refineries, get those first capture rewards with a quick start. Spread your power players and supporting players evenly to optimise their collective strength.&#x20;

The key here is to plan to capture and hold at least one hospital and one military base for their event buffs (faster cheaper healing and attack buffs). Moving away from the refineries to ensure you can take and hold one of each , then have a plan to deny the enemy theirs - they will be planning to do the same to you!

​

![DWS BGB Objective planner](/images/bgb-objective-targets.png)

The Crews option brings up the Objectives list and the crews you are going to allocate. This helps you spread the power players and support players across key strategic objectives, to both get those first captures, holding bonus and obviously the healing and battle buffs.&#x20;

###### Objective Timeline

* Refineries & Oil Collection
* Hospitals & Military Bases
* Golden Ball - Rare Earth
